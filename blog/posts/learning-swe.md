## Everything AI

My professor at Duke once told the class that when he was an undergraduate, studying
machine learning, he never could have imagined the conversations he overheard in a cafe
would be about AI like we have today.
Indeed, everywhere I go it seems like I will never escape the word AI.
I see billboards have advertisements of products that adopted AI, social media full of AI doom and glory,
friends and family talking about it during dinner, and professors and scholars trying to ride on the hype
and get their publications published.
